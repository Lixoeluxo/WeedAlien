Northern Lights

Indica Strain

A cross between Afghani and Thai indicas, the Northern Lights strain is 95% indica and just 5% Sativa. Its THC content hovers between 16% and 21% while its CBD levels are practically nonexistent.

Afghan Kush

Indica Strain

Afghan Kush is an indica cannabis variety originating from the famous Hindu Kush mountain range that spans along the Afghan-Pakistan border. It is renowned for embodying the best of the indica world; big, heavy yields of resinous buds that produce that iconic body stone many users love.

Bruce Benner

Sativa Strain

Bruce Banner is a hybrid strain resulting from crossing OG Kush and Strawberry Diesel. It is Sativa-dominated cannabis (60% Sativa and 40% Indica) bred by Dark Horse Genetics. This is a highly potent strain with an average THC value of 25%, but Bruce Banner #3 has recorded a whopping 30% THC

White Widow

Indica Strain

White Widow is an Indica-dominant hybrid cannabis strain that was originally bred by Green House Seeds (the Green House Seed Company) in the Netherlands in the 1990s. It was created as a cross between two landrace strains - a Brazilian Sativa and a South Indian Indica
